Research Objectives

The major implication of this work is to develop and apply an Artificial Intelligence-based precision agriculture system to Indian agricultural practice. Based on AI, IoT, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Remote Sensing, our goal is to improve resource utilization efficiency, crop disease identification, yield predictions, and decision-making processes.
